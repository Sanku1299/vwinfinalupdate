Intro

Founded in 2017, backed by decades of industry experience, V-Win Express International has grown into a trusted logistics partner and a rising name in the courier and freight industry, headquartered in Mumbai.

We provide a diverse suite of logistics solutions tailored for clients with both domestic and international networks. From express deliveries across India to specialized shipments to the MENA region, we deliver reliability, speed, and care—every single time.

Expressing Values.
Delivering Commitments.

At V-Win Express International, we don’t just deliver parcels — we deliver trust, urgency, and reliability. Rooted in the heart of Mumbai and reaching across the globe, our logistics solutions are built to support both businesses and individual customers with unmatched efficiency.
Whether it’s a time-sensitive domestic shipment, eCommerce delivery, or an international consignment to the MENA region, we make sure your shipment arrives on time, every time.

What Sets Us Apart

1

Last to Pick Up. First to Deliver.

We understand urgency. That’s why we schedule pickups later than most and ensure deliveries land ahead of schedule, especially for metro and Tier I & II cities.

2

Specialized MENA Network

Our international operations are finely tuned for the Middle East — UAE, KSA, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, Kuwait, and Iran. Through a well-established agent network, we ensure pre-clearance and smooth delivery every time.

3

Strong Delivery Backbone

With direct loading, nationwide air connectivity, and partnerships with major global couriers, we offer powerful coverage for imports, exports, sea, and air freight services.

4

Critical Cargo? We Handle It.

From HAZ/DGR shipments to temperature-sensitive packages, we coordinate closely with airlines and verify documentation before acceptance
